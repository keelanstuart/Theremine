Theremine is an application that lets you turn your PC into a theremin by using a LEAP Motion Controller to track your hand(s).

Hold your hand above the sensor and a tone will be generated that corresponds to the distance from the sensor. The frequency generated can be tweaked with a minimum, maximum, and step value... and the waveform shape can be selected from sine, square, triangle, and sawtooth. Volume is modulated with your palm -- palm facing the sensor is full volume while palm facing the side is silent.
